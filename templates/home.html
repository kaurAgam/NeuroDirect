INFORMATION

AD (Alzheimer Disease)

Alzheimer's disease (AD) is a progressive and irreversible neurodegenerative disorder that primarily affects the brain, leading to a decline in memory, cognitive function, and the ability to perform everyday activities.

CN (Cognitively Normal)

CN refers to individuals who exhibit normal cognitive function without significant memory or thinking problems.

MCI (Mild Cognitive Impairment)

MCI is a condition that falls between normal age-related cognitive decline and more serious conditions like Alzheimer's disease.

Ayurvedic Remedies

Panchakarma

Panchakarma, a detoxifying Ayurvedic therapy, aids Alzheimer's management by eliminating toxins and balancing body energies. Vamana, Virechana, Basti, and Nasya techniques remove impurities, supporting better cognitive function and mental clarity in patients. It complements herbs and lifestyle adjustments for comprehensive treatment.

Herbal Remedy

Ayurveda prescribes herbs like Brahmi, Vacha, and Ashwagandha to prevent brain degeneration and enhance cognitive abilities in Alzheimer's patients. These herbs target specific body channels, nourishing the brain and supporting mental agility. Combined with lifestyle changes, they form an integral part of Alzheimer's treatment in Ayurveda.

Ayurvedic Therapy

Ayurvedic therapies such as Rasayana, Shiro Basti, and Abhyanga offer holistic approaches to Alzheimer's management. These therapies aid in rejuvenating the mind, reducing stress, and enhancing mental clarity. Complemented by internal medications and suitable activities, they contribute to comprehensive treatment strategies.
