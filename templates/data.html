AD (Alzheimer's Disease)

Alzheimer's disease is a progressive neurodegenerative disorder that affects the brain, primarily causing problems with memory, thinking, and behavior

CN (Cognitively Normal)

CN refers to individuals who exhibit normal cognitive function without significant memory or thinking problems.

MCI (Mild Cognitive Impairment)

MCI is a condition that falls between normal age-related cognitive decline and more serious conditions like Alzheimer's disease.
